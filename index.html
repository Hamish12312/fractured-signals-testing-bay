About Us

Fractured Signals is a UK-based drum & bass project built around the creative partnership between SH3M and Guy Oakland.

SH3M brings fresh energy as a DJ and producer — performing at Truck Festival, Y Not? Festival, and holding regular sets at O2 Academy Oxford — crafting bass-heavy rhythms and developing new material for release. Guy Oakland, formerly a solo artist with a catalogue of originals and remixes, adds production depth and musical experience drawn from years of writing across genres.

Formed after crossing paths as colleagues, the pair combined their ideas, influences, and experience to create Fractured Signals. Rooted in Oxfordshire, the project represents the intersection of their musical identities — blending together many styles with a shared passion for drum & bass.

Fractured Signals isn’t just a collaboration; it’s a progression. A merging of backgrounds. A focused effort to build a distinct, evolving DnB sound driven by creativity, precision, and genuine love for the craft.
